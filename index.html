About Me

Hello, I'm Oscar. I'm a teenager passionate about ecommerce, coding, and graphic design—ways to bring my ideas to life. Since childhood, I've envisioned apps and businesses, and I've pursued skills to build them.

When I was 11, I built a website using HTML, CSS, and JS loaded with different games for my classmates to play. The games only appeared when holding down the Z key on our school laptops so that if you released it, the screen would switch to a random article - making it looked like you were doing work in class. The website required a password to enter, and so I would charge my classmates a coin for a 'license' to use the website.

Needless to say it was a success - and the beginning of my realisation that there were real people who wanted what I made.

Today, I focus on:

  • Studying e-commerce & economics
  • Building UI/UX-friendly applications
  • IoT/Robotics (Raspberry Pi, Arduino)
  • Cybersecurity research
  • Freelance graphic design
